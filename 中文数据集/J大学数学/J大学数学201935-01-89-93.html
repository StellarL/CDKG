    摘    要：

    通常的微积分教材中, 可以计算的不定积分主要包括有理函数、三角函数有理式和某些含根式的函数.受几个具体函数积分方法的启发, 证明了一类含有指数函数的积分公式, 该结果包含了微积分教材中用常规积分方法较难推演的一类含指数函数的积分.

    关键词：

    指数函数; 不定积分; 积分方法; 积分公式;

    The Research on a Class of Indefinite Integrals with Exponential Functions

    LIU Wei-ping BI Zhi-wei LIU Ji-cheng

    School of Mathematics and Statistics, Huazhong University of Science and Technology Wenhua College

    Abstract：

    In the usual calculus textbooks, the indefinite integrals which can be integrated are mainly rational functions, rational expressions of trigonometric functions and some functions with radical expressions.Inspired by integral methods of several specific functions, we prove the integral formulae for a class of indefinite integrals with exponential functions.The results include a class of integrals with exponential functions which are difficultly integrated by using the conventional methods in calculus textbooks.

    Keyword：

    exponential function; indefinite integral; integral method; integral formula;

    Received： 2018-06-18
