    GUO Xu ZHENG Jun

    School of Information Science & Technology, Southwest Jiaotong University School of Mathematics, Southwest Jiaotong University

    Abstract：

    Two well-known integral inequalities have been studied and extended to the case of higher order by integration by parts, Taylor formula, and differential mean value theorem, etc.An example on Lyapunov inequality and eigenvalue problems has been provided to illustrate the application of the obtained results.
