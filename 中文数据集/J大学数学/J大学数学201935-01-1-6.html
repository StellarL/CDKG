    摘    要：

    介绍了p-EP元的概念, 这是一类特殊的EP元, 研究了p-EP元的性质与刻画, 并给出了一个EP元成为p-EP元的若干条件.

    关键词：

    群可逆元; MP可逆元; EP可逆元; p-EP可逆元;

    作者简介： 潘梦雅 (1994-) , 女, 硕士在读, 基础数学专业.Email:mx120170236@yzu.edu.cn; *叶舒依 (1993-) , 女, 硕士在读, 基础数学专业.Email:552486414@qq.com;

    A Class of EP Elements in Associative Ring

    PAN Meng-ya LV Qiu-yue YE Shu-yi

    School of Mathematics, Yangzhou University

    Abstract：

    We introduce the notion of p-E Pelements, which is a special class of EP elements.Some properties and characterizations of p-E Pelements are given and the conditions of EP elements being p-E Pelements are discussed.

    Keyword：

    group invertible element; MP invertible element; EP element; p-EP element;
