    太赫兹正六边形光子晶体光纤的液体传感（英文）

    摘    要：

    设计了一种分别填充水、酒精和苯炔等边三角形空气孔纤芯、包层空气孔排列为正六边形结构、以环烯烃共聚物作为基底材料的光子晶体光纤, 利用有限元法模拟了0. 3～1. 5 THz频带内的液体传感特性。结果表明, 设计的光子晶体光纤的灵敏度系数很高, 当纤芯空气孔直径d1=2. 4μm、包层空气填充比为0. 3时, 苯炔在频率0. 3 THz的相对灵敏性系数最高为37. 63%, 在1. 5 THz, 限制损耗的数量级可以降至10-7。

    关键词：

    光子晶体光纤; 太赫兹; 传感; 灵敏度; 限制损耗;

    Liquid Sensing of Hexagonal Photonic Crystal Fibers for Terahertz Wave

    DING Run-qi HOU Shang-lin LEI Jing-li WANG Dao-bin LI Xiao-xiao WANG Hui-qin CAO Ming-hua

    School of Science, Lanzhou University of Technology School of Computer and Communication, Lanzhou University of Technology

    Abstract：

    A photonic crystal fiber with the cladding of circular air holes in hexagonal lattice structure and the core of circular air holes in equilateral triangle lattice structure based on cyclic-olefin copolymer is proposed, the liquid sensing characteristics of the PCF over 0. 3-1. 5 THz bands are investigated by finite element method. The proposed PCF demonstrates an ultrahigh sensitivity coefficient of 37. 63% at 0. 3 THz, and the confinement loss is low as the order 10-7 at frequency of 1. 5 THz. The results offer reference for designing novel liquid sensors based on PCF at terahertz band.

    Keyword：

    photonic crystal fiber; terahertz; sensing; sensitivity; confinement loss;

    1 Introduction

    Terahertz wave is defined by the frequency range of 0.1-10 THz (1012cycles per second) , which situated between infrared light and microwave radiation[1,2].The terahertz (THz) region of the electromagnetic spectrum has proven to be one of the most elusive and is resistant to the techniques commonly employed in these well-established neighboring bands.But for recent decades, THz technology has attracted increasing interests due to various applications ranging from spectroscopy, security screening, medical imaging to sensing[3,4], and becomes a quite significant interdisciplinary field that provides opportunities for national economic development and national security.However, high atmospheric absorption constrains applications and funding for THz science.

    Due to flexible designing by tailoring the parameters of photonic crystal fiber (PCF) , photonic crystal fiber is becoming the excellent medium for THz wave transmission[5,6].Conventional photonic crystal fiber consists of an arbitrary order of tiny air holes running along the entire length of the fiber or periodic array, while the core is periodically destroyed of defect formation.The material and structural properties of PCF are important for guiding lights.PCF technology allows for the accurate tuning of the propagation properties of fiber by tailoring air hole shape, size and their positions, different characteristics of PCF can be achieved by changing geometry parameters.At PCF, sensing is the interaction between passing light and analyte which are alterable by varying the frequency, intensity, wavelength, phase and polarization state of light etc.The properties of PCF with air holes filled with liquid can be changed by varying properties of liquid induced by outer environment.Sen et al.[7]presented a porous cored hexagonal PCF with liquid filled air holes exhibiting high relative sensitivity and lower confinement at operating wavelength of 0.8-1.8μm.Ahmed et al.[8]designed an octagonal PCF for liquid sensing which demonstrates lower confinement loss and higher sensitivity at wavelength of 0.52-2μm.

    It should be noted that the analysis of the sensing characteristics of photonic crystal fibers is mainly focused on the communication band[8].But for terahertz band, the conventional fiber has higher loss, and the air holes are too small to inject the liquid into them.Thus it needs to find new matrix material to replace the pure silicon, and tailor the parameters of PCF to reduce its confinement loss and other transmission properties.

    In this paper, a PCF with cladding of circular air holes in hexagonal lattice structure for terahertz wave is presented, its sensitivity and confinement loss are investigated for sensing applications.The whole PCF is based on cyclic-olefin copolymer (COC) [9]whose refractive index is regard as constant within the frequency range of 0.1-2.0 THz.The designed PCF shows not only the relative sensitivity coefficient improved to 37.63%for benzyne, but also confinement loss of the order 10-7at frequency range of 0.3-1.5 THz.

    2 Design Methodology

    The cross section of the presented PCF is shown in Fig.1.The cladding consists of circular air holes forming hexagonal lattice structure, while the core has equilateral triangle lattice structure.Liquid, such as water, ethanol and benzyne whose refractive indices are 1.330, 1.354, 1.366[10]respectively, is filled in the air holes of core region.COC has been utilized as the background material for the proposed PCF and refractive index is regard as constant within the frequency range of 0.1-2.0 THz.It is critical that the COC is insensitive to humidity and biosensing with low loss and low dispersion for the terahertz frequency range compared to other polymers.The hole-to-hole distance in the cladding is denoted byΛ, the air hole diameters in the core and cladding are denoted by d1and d2respectively, and set d2/Λ=0.3.

    3 Results and Discussion

    Fig.2 shows that the fundamental mode field profile of the proposed PCF for the cladding air filling ratio (AFF) d2/Λ=0.3 and d1=1.4μm.It can be seen that when water, ethanol and benzyne are filled in the air holes of the core, the mode fields at 1.0 THz are all tightly bounded in the core region.

    The effect of filled-liquid on the relative sensitivity coefficient was simulated from Eq. (2) and (3) as shown in Fig.3 (a) .It can be found that relative sensitivity increases with increasing the analytes refractive index at terahertz bands, when d1=1.4μm and d2/Λ=0.3, the relative sensitivity is very high as 36.59%, 33.69%and 31.46%of benzyne, ethano and water-filled at 0.3 THz, respectively.

    Confinement loss of the proposed PCF as a function of frequency for different supplementary is shown in Fig.3 (b) .It is observed that for same filled-liquid, αCLdecreases with the frequency increasing.Because light is more tightly confined in the porous core, the confinement loss is reduced at high frequency.

    In order to investigate the influence of diameter of core air holes on sensitivity and loss, the relative sensitivity with different frequency is depicted in Fig.4 (a) .It can be seen that while d1=2.4μm and d2/Λ=0.3, the relative sensitivity is as high as37.63%, 34.89%and 32.52%for benzyne, ethano and water-filled at 0.3 THz, respectively.In addition, the three kinds of liquid reveal the minimum sensitivity of 19.48%, 17.27%and 14.89%for n=1.33, 1.354, 1.366 at 1.5 THz, respectively.At a given value of frequency, the relative sensitivity of d1=2.4μm is greater than d1=1.4μm.It also can be seen that with the diameter of core air holes increasing, the relative sensitivity is increasing consequently.

    Fig.4 (b) shows the confinement loss of the proposed PCF as a function of frequency.We can see from the figure that the confinement loss is relatively low within 0.3-1.5 THz.For a given frequency, the confinement loss decreases with increasing the diameter of core air holes.It can be seen that the lowest confinement loss in the core air holes filled with benzyne is about 4.62×10-7dB/m for d1=2.4μm and d2/Λ=0.3.

    4 Conclusion

    In summary, a new equilateral triangular core optical crystal fiber has been proposed and its relative sensitivity and confinement loss are characterized for three filled liquids at terahertz wave.A thorough numerical investigation has been performed to obtain very high relative sensitivity 37.63%for benzyne filled at 0.3 THz with d2/Λ=0.3 and d1=2.4μm, besides, very low confinement loss 4.62×10-7dB·m-1for water filled at 1.5 THz.
