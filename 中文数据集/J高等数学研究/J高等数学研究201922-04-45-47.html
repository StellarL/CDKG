    摘    要：

    本文通过取凸包与闭包, 利用关系正常法, Lebesgue测度和均匀连续映射, 讨论欧氏空间的集值映射的不动点, 获得了一些结果.

    关键词：

    集值映射; 连续性; 不动点;

    作者简介： 林一星 (1940-) , 男, 福建龙岩人, 高级讲师, 从事集值分析与测度论研究, Email:keke2025@163.com;

    收稿日期：2018-05-25

    Fixed Points of Set-Valued Mappings in Euclidean Space

    LIN Yixing

    The Senior Middle School of Longyan (Normal)

    Abstract：

    In this paper, by taking the convex and closed hulls and using the relationship-normal method, Lebesgue measures, and uniform continuous mappings, we discuss the fixed points of set-valued mappings in Euclidean Space, and obtain some results.

    Keyword：

    set-valued mapping; continuity; fixed point;
