    本文在实数域中, 利用矩阵的Jordan标准形与相似变换, 给出了求矩阵m次方根的一种解法, 并针对矩阵求奇数次方根, 证明了此时矩阵方根的唯一性.最后给出计算实例.

    On Uniqueness of Odd Roots of a Square Matrix in Real Field

    WANG Lei GUO Xinyu FENG Weijie

    School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering, Beihang University

    Abstract：

    By using the Jordan standard form and the similarity transform of matrix, this paper gives a solution to find the m-th root of a square matrix in the real field. In particular, we show that there is a unique solution for the odd roots of a square matrix in the real field. Finally, two examples are given to verify our results.

    Keyword：

    m-th root of a square matrix; Jordan standard form; similarity transform of matrix;

    Received： 2018-11-06

    引言

    在工程应用中, 我们会遇到求一个矩阵m次方根的问题. 如在多载波调制技术中, 基于矩阵开方来设计盲信道估计算法[1]. 文献[2,3,4]等讨论了在复数域上矩阵m次方根的存在性问题, 可以看出所求的m次方根通常不唯一. 矩阵在复数域上求方根计算较为复杂, 而在实数域中, 满足一定条件下, 所求的m次方根具有唯一性, 从而使问题的计算过程与结果得以化简.
