定理 (自创题,2017.08.17)设x1、x2、x3、x4∈R,则

x1x2(x32+x42)+x3x4(x12+x22)≤14(x1+x2)2(x3+x4)2,

当且仅当x1=x2或x3=x4时,取等号.

证明 (x1+x2)2(x3+x4)2-4x1x2(x32+x42)-4x3x4(x12+x22)=(x12+x22+2x1x2)(x3+x4)2-4x1x2(x32+x42)-4x3x4(x12+x22)=(x12+x22)(x3+x4)2-4x3x4(x12+x22)+2x1x2(x3+x4)2-4x1x2(x32+x32)=(x12+x22)(x3-x4)2-2x1x2(x3-x4)2=(x1-x2)2(x3-x4)2≥0,

即得原式,易知当且仅当x1=x2或x3=x4时,取等号.
